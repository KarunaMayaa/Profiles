Personality Overview Amberdawn is the type of cat to put herself in her own position of power: as the Clan's mother. As an older cat, she tends to feel responsible for the younger generations, wanting to be there to support them. She spends a lot of time in the nursery, even though her kits are all grown, and sometimes she may get a bit too involved with other's lives. She is also very stubborn, and has a no-nonsense attitude, so it can be very difficult to get your way with her.
