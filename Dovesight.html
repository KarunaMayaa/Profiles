VOICE
Voice Claim: Vereesa Windrunner
Dovesight's voice is soft, and sounds more mature than what her age would suggest. She doesn't yell often, so when she does there can be a rasp to her otherwise quiet voice. There is often a bit of a sweet tone, as if her voice was laced with honey, yet there is never any sting.

STYLE
Dovesight is usually rather unassuming, keeping her head low as she walks through the crowd. She spends most of her time hunched over, whether it be treating her patients, or simply in the way she walks. Her strides are usually long, and her movements fast, as if she is running from something.
